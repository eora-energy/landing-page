1. Introduction

At EORA Energy, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you subscribe to our newsletter.

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Swiss data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the following information:

  • Name - To personalize our communications
  • Email address - To send you our newsletter
  • Subscription date - For our records

That's all. We don't collect any other personal data, tracking information, or analytics beyond what's necessary for newsletter delivery.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Send you our newsletter with EORA Energy updates and energy industry insights
  • Personalize the content by addressing you by name
  • Maintain our subscriber list

We will never:

  • Sell your email address or name to third parties
  • Share your information with marketers or advertisers
  • Use your data for any purpose other than sending you our newsletter
  • Send you spam or unwanted communications

4. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), a GDPR-compliant email service provider. Brevo uses industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure data centers with physical and digital protection
  • Regular security audits and compliance certifications

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a subscriber, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Unsubscribe at any time

6. How to Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any newsletter email
  • Emailing us at info@eora-energy.ch with your request

Your data will be removed from our system within 30 days of your unsubscribe request.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. After you unsubscribe, your data is deleted within 30 days.

8. International Data Transfers

Our email service provider (Brevo) may store data on servers located in the European Union and other countries. All data transfers comply with GDPR requirements and use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Children's Privacy

Our newsletter is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has subscribed, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Significant changes will be communicated to subscribers via email.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, want to exercise your GDPR rights, or have concerns about your data, please contact us at:

Email: info@eora-energy.ch
